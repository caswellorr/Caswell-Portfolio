About Me

My name is Caswell Lee Orr. It's a family name. I believe it's Irish, but I'm not entirely sure.

During the pandemic I bought a sailboat in California and moved onto it. I did it because it seemed like the perfect time to do so. I had little money and an old boat that needed some updates. One of which was an updated navigation system. So I scoured the internet for DIY navigation because the package systems cost thousands of dollars. I came across a Reddit page that described building your own system using a Raspberry Pi, a mini comoputer, and a bit of programming. Hmm, I thought, this looks cool. And it's only two hundred bucks for all the gear.
