About Me

The company I worked for went under after the pandemic hit. For years I dreamt of buying and living on a sailboat. I saved money and took sailing courses on my time off and waited for the right opportunity to pursue this dream. With an open schedule beginning in March 2020, I went for it. I bought a sailboat in Long Beach, California, and moved onto it. After fixing it up and acquainting myself I sailed north to Ventura where I settled down for a while.

To pay the bills and feed myself I played music for cash at a local bar in the harbor on the weekends. After a few months, the marina informed me that I wasn't allowed to live full time in the harbor, so I sailed twenty miles out to Santa Cruz Island for a few days a week to comply with the marina's rules. This introduced me to computer programming for the first time. I experienced a familiar satisfaction in building and creating, so I looked further into this new world of computer programming, seeking to learn more.
